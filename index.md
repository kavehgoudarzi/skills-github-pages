# Welcome to My Digital Space

Hello! I'm **Kaveh Goudarzi**, a business consultant and innovation ecosystem developer with over a decade of experience building infrastructure in digital industries. 

Currently, I serve as the **CEO and co-founder of Jahesh (Innovation and Acceleration Center)** and am a **member of the Board of the Accelerator Commission of the VC Association**. My core mission is to bridge the gap between industry, academia, and startups.

## What I Do

My work revolves around the concept of **Innovation Ecosystem as a Service (IEaaS)**. I specialize in designing corporate innovation hubs, developing university-industry collaboration models, and fostering environments where digital economies can thrive. 

Some of my current and recent focuses include:

* **Virtual Innovation Hubs:** Leading the development of *Jahesh Park*, a 2D gamified virtual world leveraging AI and blockchain for professional networking and startup discovery.
* **Physical Infrastructure:** Managing large-scale physical ecosystem projects like the *Tehran Innovation Bridge* twin towers.
* **Strategic Consulting:** Aligning venture capital, government stakeholders, and emerging tech to build sustainable innovation pipelines.

## About This Blog

With a foundation in Computer Science and years of hands-on experience in the startup and VC landscape, I created this space to share my thoughts on:
* The future of the digital economy and web3 technologies.
* Insights into venture capital and accelerator management.
* Case studies on building effective corporate innovation structures.

Feel free to explore my posts, check out my ongoing projects, or connect with me to discuss the future of innovation infrastructure!
